Notes

  • Assignment weights, amounts and extensions from each master's wiki page; drop tables parsed from every monster's wiki page by build-data.mjs (source links in the repo). Data snapshot: .
  • Loot per kill values tradeables at max(GE mid-price, high alch). Clue scrolls, champion scrolls and keys are tertiary: shown in the breakdown but never counted in GP totals.
  • "Uniques" chance is 1 − Π(1−p)N over the chosen monster's rare drops for your task amount.
  • Points: long-run average per completed task including the every-10th/50th/100th/250th/1000th milestone bonuses; skipping costs 30 and does not advance the streak. Turael resets are not modelled here.
  • Konar tasks pay brimstone keys (rate per monster from its wiki page); Krystilia tasks pay Larran's keys (1/floor(99−(cb−81)×49/269) at cb 81+). Konar's per-location restrictions aren't modelled for loot.
  • Krystilia's revenant task uses on-task, unskulled revenant rates — see the revenant calculator for the full on-task vs off-task analysis.
  • Boss tasks: pick which boss you'd kill in the Kill dropdown; amount is the wiki's 3–35 roll (Combat Achievements can raise it).
  • GWD room kills: Kree'arra + all three minions count as aviansies (4/room); K'ril + Tstanon Karlak + Zakl'n Gritch count as greater demons (3/room, Balfrug is looted but doesn't count). source
  • Superiors: 1/200 spawn per on-task kill with Bigger and Badder; each gives 3 bonus base-table rolls and rolls two unique tables at 1/(200 − (req+55)²/125) — heart is 1/8 of the first table, eternal gem is the whole second.
  • Bracelets: slaughter = 25% of kills don't count (×4/3 kills per task); expeditious = 25% count double (×0.8). Charges/cost not modelled.
  • Ironman banked XP covers gems, herbs (choose your potion), bones (bury/gilded/chaos), dragonhide, smithable bars and ensouled heads. Seeds/farming, secondaries gathering and enchanting magic XP are not counted.
